Fierce Lion Creation Recipe

3rd Guardian - Fierce Lion

Items required for the Combination

  • 2nd Guardian +11
  • Lion Seal
  • Jewel of Bless, Jewel of Soul, Jewel of Chaos, Jewel of Creation

Steps to Create Fierce Lion

  1. Mount Guardian Level +11 or superior
  2. Fierce Lion Seal can be obtain for 50.000 Ruud on the Npc "Priest"
  3. Jewel of Bless *20, Jewel of Soul*20, Jewel of Chaos*1, Jewel of Creation*1
  4. Go to Lorencia and look for the Npc (122,11) "Trainer"

(1) 3rd Guardian 'Fierce Lion', 'Fierce Lion(Rare)' has been added.

① The attributes of ‘Fierce Lion (Rare)’ are the same as ‘Fierce Lion’, but it has a different color and a lower probability of being acquired.

(2) The 3rd Guardian is a mount that is equipped in the inventory.

(3) The 3rd Guardian can be acquired through Upgrade Combination of 2nd Guardian.
