Earring of Wrath Left and Right. How to get and level Up

How to get it

  • NPC Moss el "Mastery Accesory Box"
  • The "Mastery Accessory Box" can be opened with the Jewel of Bless and you can get the Earring with a certain probability.
  • The Rings and Pendant that dropped in (Elbeland, Lost Tower, Atlans and Tarkan) can now only be acquired through the NPC Moss.

Earring level up

  1. Earring can be leveled up through the NPC Chaos Goblin.
  2. Leveling up the Earring will require the "Lapidary Stone" and zen.
  3. The Earring will not be destroyed if you fail to level up.
